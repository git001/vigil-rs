## When do you need a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if **any** of the following apply:

- You embed vigil-rs in a **closed-source product** that you distribute or sell.

- Your organization has a **policy against AGPL** software (e.g. internal legal
  requirement).

## What is free under AGPL-3.0?

- Using vigil-rs inside your own infrastructure (containers, VMs, bare metal)
  without distributing it to third parties.
- Open-source projects that are themselves licensed under a compatible
  copyleft license.
- Evaluation, testing, and development.
